DATASET METADATA: Heart Failure Prediction
SOURCE: Kaggle (Heart Failure Clinical Records)
---------------------------------------------------------

COLUMN DESCRIPTIONS:

1. age
   - Description: Age of the patient.
   - Unit: Years

2. anaemia
   - Description: Decrease of red blood cells or hemoglobin.
   - Values: 0 = No, 1 = Yes

3. creatinine_phosphokinase
   - Description: Level of the CPK enzyme in the blood.
   - Unit: mcg/L

4. diabetes
   - Description: If the patient has diabetes.
   - Values: 0 = No, 1 = Yes

5. ejection_fraction
   - Description: Percentage of blood leaving the heart at each contraction.
   - Unit: Percentage (%)

6. high_blood_pressure
   - Description: If the patient has hypertension.
   - Values: 0 = No, 1 = Yes

7. platelets
   - Description: Platelets in the blood.
   - Unit: kiloplatelets/mL

8. serum_creatinine
   - Description: Level of serum creatinine in the blood.
   - Unit: mg/dL

9. serum_sodium
   - Description: Level of serum sodium in the blood.
   - Unit: mEq/L

10. sex
    - Description: Gender of the patient.
    - Values: 0 = Female, 1 = Male

11. smoking
    - Description: If the patient smokes.
    - Values: 0 = No, 1 = Yes

12. time
    - Description: Follow-up period (duration of observation).
    - Unit: Days

13. DEATH_EVENT (Target Variable)
    - Description: If the patient died during the follow-up period.
    - Values: 0 = No (Survived), 1 = Yes (Deceased)
